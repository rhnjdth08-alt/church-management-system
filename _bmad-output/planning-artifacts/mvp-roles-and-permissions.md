# MVP Roles and Permissions

## Roles
- Admin: Full access to users, member records, attendance, giving, fundraising, and reports.
- Pastor/Ministry Leader: View and manage attendance, divisions, and reporting for ministry activities.
- Treasurer: Manage giving and fundraising records, campaign progress, and financial summaries.
- Class Leader: View and update attendance for assigned divisions such as Sunday School, Youth, or Adult classes.
- Staff Member: View member data, send communications, and manage basic event tasks.

## Permission Summary
### Admin
- Create, edit, and deactivate member records
- Manage divisions and class assignments
- Record attendance and event data
- Manage giving and fundraising campaigns
- View all reports and dashboards
- Manage user accounts and roles

### Pastor/Ministry Leader
- View member and division information
- Record or review attendance by division
- View ministry reports and attendance trends
- Manage event-related communication

### Treasurer
- Record donations and pledges
- Manage fundraising campaigns for the church building
- View financial summaries and campaign progress
- Export reports for leadership review

### Class Leader
- Record attendance for assigned division
- View class attendance history
- View division-level reports

### Staff Member
- Search and view member information
- Send announcements and follow-ups
- Manage basic event tasks
- View limited reports
