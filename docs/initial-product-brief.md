# Church Management System — Initial Product Brief

## Purpose
Provide a practical digital solution for church staff and leadership to manage members, attendance, giving, events, and communication from a single, maintainable system.

## Problem Statement
Church teams often rely on spreadsheets, paper forms, and disconnected tools to track membership, attendance, financial giving, event participation, and volunteer communications. This creates duplicated work, inconsistent records, slow follow-up, and poor visibility across ministry activities.

## Target Users
- Church administrators and office staff
- Pastors and ministry leaders
- Volunteer coordinators
- Members and attendees

## Primary Goals
1. Centralize member and family records with contact, demographic, and participation history.
2. Track attendance and event participation reliably, with simple check-in and reporting.
3. Record giving and donations in a way that supports financial stewardship and contribution reporting.
4. Manage church events and RSVPs so ministry leaders can plan and communicate effectively.
5. Improve communication across members, groups, and volunteers through centralized messages and notices.

## Core Features
- Member Directory
  - Create and update people, households, and family relationships
  - Store contact details, tags, and ministry involvement
- Attendance & Check-In
  - Record attendance for services, classes, and events
  - Support quick check-in at the door and follow-up workflows for absences
- Giving Management
  - Log gifts, tithes, and offerings by person or household
  - Generate contribution summaries for reporting and statements
- Events & Calendar
  - Create events with registration or RSVP tracking
  - Share event details, volunteer requirements, and reminders
- Communication Hub
  - Send announcements, reminders, and follow-ups
  - Track outreach and responses
- Reporting & Insights
  - Provide dashboards for attendance trends, giving patterns, and engagement metrics
  - Export data for leadership review

## Success Metrics
- Reduction in time spent maintaining membership and attendance lists
- Faster follow-up for new visitors and volunteer recruitment
- Accurate, auditable giving records for church finance
- Increased event participation through better communication
- Higher confidence among staff that church records are current and consistent

## Assumptions
- The church wants a maintainable, low-complexity system rather than an overly custom enterprise solution.
- Data privacy and security are important for member records.
- The initial implementation should focus on core administrative workflows first.
